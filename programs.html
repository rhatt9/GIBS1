Special Programmes

  • The Art and Science of Entrepreneurship
    Learn the entrepreneurial mindset, risks, and rewards of starting a business, and how to create a business model canvas.
  • Strategic Business Planning and Management
    Understand the importance of business plans, strategy frameworks, and how to present a professional business plan.
  • Fund Raising and Financial Management for Business
    Explore techniques for raising funds, choosing capital providers, and managing post-funding relationships.
  • Introduction to International Transport and Logistics
    Learn the basics of sea and air freight logistics, packaging procedures, and cross-border trade operations.
  • Meeting Standards in the Agri-Food Sector
    Understand food safety standards, HACCP certification, and compliance for agri-food production.
  • Building Competitiveness in Trade Services
    Discover strategies to enhance trade services competitiveness and the role of digital technology in trade.
  • Introduction to Supply Chain Management
    Learn supply chain strategies, key competencies, and trade-offs to improve operational effectiveness.
  • Export Marketing Strategy within the AfCFTA Framework
    Develop export marketing strategies and craft messages to target international customers effectively.
  • Introduction to E-Commerce & Digital Marketing
    Explore e-commerce opportunities, market research, and strategies for launching an online sales channel.
  • Export Business Generation and Sales
    Learn sales and negotiation tactics, value proposition creation, and preparation for international sales meetings.
  • Introduction to Cross-Border Contracts
    Understand cross-border contracts, legal concepts, and terms like force majeure and compliance requirements.
  • Monitoring and Evaluation Programme
    Gain skills in designing M&E systems, tracking performance indicators, and evaluating project outcomes.
